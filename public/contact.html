Frequently asked questions

Is Scholix free to use?

Yes, Scholix is completely free for all students. We sustain the platform through non-intrusive advertising via Google AdSense and voluntary community contributions. There are no premium tiers or paywalls.

Which universities does Scholix support?

Currently, Scholix supports Lovely Professional University (LPU) and IIT Madras BS Program. Each university has a dedicated gateway with customized study materials, campus-specific tools, and institutional resources. More universities are being added regularly.

How do I upload study materials?

You can contribute study materials through the Content Library section after creating an account. Materials go through admin review before being published to ensure quality and accuracy. You can upload notes, PYQs, lab records, and other academic resources.

Is my data safe on Scholix?

Yes. We use Supabase's encrypted infrastructure with row-level security policies. All connections are SSL/TLS encrypted, and we implement session token verification for secure API access. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.

How does the AI Quiz Generator work?

Our quiz generator uses Google Gemini AI to create custom questions from any subject topic. You can specify the difficulty level, number of questions, and topic focus. The system tracks your performance over time with XP-based progression and detailed analytics.

Can I delete my account and data?

Yes. You can request complete account deletion and data removal through the Settings page or by contacting us at anunayarvind@gmail.com. We comply with GDPR and CCPA privacy regulations.
