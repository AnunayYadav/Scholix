About us

Everything students need, in one platform

Scholix is a free, AI-powered student utility hub that transforms the university experience. Built by students, for students — bringing study materials, smart tools, and campus resources into a single platform.

Our mission

We believe every student deserves free, high-quality academic resources and intelligent tools to succeed. Scholix eliminates the fragmentation of student life — no more juggling WhatsApp groups for notes, random websites for PYQs, and spreadsheets for CGPA tracking.

Platform features

📚

Content Library

Access 200+ study materials including handwritten notes, PYQs, and records organized by program, semester, and subject.

🧠

AI Quiz Generator

Generate custom quizzes from any subject using AI. Track performance with analytics, earn XP, and level up.

📊

CGPA Calculator

Calculate your CGPA, forecast performance, and share beautiful report cards. Supports multiple grading systems.

✅

Attendance Tracker

Monitor attendance in real-time. Know how many classes you can safely skip while maintaining required percentage.

📅

Timetable Hub

Sync and manage your weekly schedule. See today's classes at a glance with real-time status indicators.

💼

Placement Prefect

AI resume analyzer powered by Google Gemini. Get feedback, ATS scores, and career preparation guidance.

🗺️

Campus Hub

Navigate your campus with ease. Find classrooms, blocks, mess menus, and important locations.

🏠

Roommate Finder

Find your perfect flatmate based on lifestyle, budget, and habits. Connect with compatible students.

🎒

Freshmen Kit

Survival guide for new students. Admission tips to campus life hacks compiled by seniors.

Technology

Built with React, TypeScript, and hosted on Vercel's edge network. Backend uses Supabase for auth, database, and secure file storage. AI features powered by Google Gemini for quiz generation and resume analysis.

Supported universities

Currently supports Lovely Professional University (LPU) and IIT Madras BS Program, with more coming. Each gateway is customized with relevant materials and campus-specific tools. Want Scholix at your university? Contact us.

Free and open

Scholix is completely free. Sustained through non-intrusive Google AdSense advertising and community contributions. No paywalls, no premium tiers — powerful tools for everyone.
